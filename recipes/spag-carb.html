Spaghetti Carbonara

Home

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a pan and cook the pancetta until crisp.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese, and season with black pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some of the cooking water.
  5. Toss the spaghetti in the pancetta and then mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper on top.

Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Carbonara!
