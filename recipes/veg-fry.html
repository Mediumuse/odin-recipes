Vegetable Stir-Fry

Home

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Broccoli florets
  • 1 Red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 Carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 Onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 1 tsp Ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the vegetables (broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, and onion) and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil over the vegetables, stirring to coat.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with rice or noodles of your choice.

Enjoy your delicious Vegetable Stir-Fry!
